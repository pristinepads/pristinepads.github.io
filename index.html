Transforming Waste into Worth — The Pristine Way

At Pristine Pads, we believe in building a cleaner, more sustainable world—starting with menstrual waste. Here's how we turn used sanitary pads into reusable resources.

01Collection & Awareness

We collaborate with institutions, communities, and partners to collect used sanitary pads through dedicated bins and awareness drives—ensuring safe and hygienic handling from the start.

Safe collection methods
Community engagement
Awareness campaigns

02Initial Sorting

Once collected, the pads are manually pre-sorted to remove contaminants and ensure only recyclable material enters the next phase. This step ensures efficiency in the separation stage.

Contaminant removal
Quality control
Pre-processing

03Rotary Separation Technology

Our custom-built rotary separator plays a vital role in segregating components. Using mechanical separation, we extract wood pulp and plastic from each pad with minimal waste and no harmful chemicals.

Chemical-free process
Component separation
Proprietary technology

04Pulp Refining

The extracted wood pulp undergoes further treatment to enhance its purity and texture. It is then repurposed into useful materials like paper products, compostable packaging, or insulation.

Quality enhancement
Material transformation
Resource optimization

05Plastic Recovery & Reuse

The separated plastic is sanitized, shredded, and processed to be reused in various industrial applications such as molded products, tiles, or even infrastructure materials.

Full sanitization
Precision shredding
Multiple applications

06Zero-Waste Vision

Our aim is zero waste. Residual waste, if any, is treated through eco-friendly disposal methods or explored for innovative reuse, ensuring we leave no harmful footprint behind.

Complete utilization
Eco-friendly disposal
Innovative solutions

With every cycle, we're not just recycling—we're empowering women, creating green jobs, and building a circular economy for menstrual hygiene management.
