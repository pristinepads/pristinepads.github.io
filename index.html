Pristine Pads and Co.

Pristine Pads and Co. is more than a recycling company; we are a movement dedicated to reshaping the way the world views sanitary waste. Founded on principles of sustainability and innovation, we work tirelessly to combat the environmental impact of discarded sanitary pads through responsible recycling practices.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What does Pristine Pads & Co. do?

We recycle used sanitary pads into eco-friendly materials, reducing waste and promoting sustainability.

2. How does the recycling process work?

We use advanced technology to safely collect, process, and convert used pads into reusable by-products with minimal environmental impact.

3. Why is recycling sanitary pads important?

Sanitary pads contribute significantly to non-biodegradable waste in landfills. Recycling them helps protect the environment and conserves resources.

4. How can I contribute to your initiative?

You can participate by properly disposing of your used pads in our collection bins or joining our community awareness programs.

5. Is your recycling process hygienic and safe?

Absolutely. Our process adheres to strict hygiene and safety standards, ensuring both environmental and public health protection.
